Garden & Griddle Privacy Policy

Publisher: Cooper Unlimited Games
Effective date: July 16, 2026

Garden & Griddle is an online cooperative game. It has no accounts, advertising, analytics, in-app purchases, or free-text chat.

Network data

The game server temporarily processes a room code, random session identifier, game actions, connection status, and IP address needed to operate and secure a two-player room. Active room data is held in server memory and normally expires after inactivity. Short-lived security logs may retain IP addresses and connection errors for up to 30 days.

Device data

Campaign progress, upgrades, settings, player name, and reconnect tokens are stored locally on your device. They are not used for advertising or profiling. Clearing app data or uninstalling removes the local copy.

Sharing

No personal data is sold. Network infrastructure providers may process connection data only to host and protect the service.

Children

The game does not request real names, email addresses, location, contacts, photos, microphone access, or camera access. Players should use a nickname.
