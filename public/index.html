How to Play

  1. Tap an empty garden plot to plant one of six crops.
  2. Pick up the blue pail, then hold a dry crop to water it.
  3. Harvest ripe crops; ingredients go straight to the shared fridge.
  4. Tap the cow for milk and the bowl to mix ten-crepe batter.
  5. Select an order ticket, then tap an empty stovetop.
  6. Tap a green ready stove to serve before the customer leaves.

Both players can do every job. Use quick pings to divide the work.
